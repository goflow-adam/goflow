  <section itemScope itemType="https://schema.org/HowTo">
    <h2 itemprop="name">How to Choose the Right Faucet</h2>
    <div itemprop="step" itemScope itemType="https://schema.org/HowToStep">
      <h3 itemprop="name">1. Style and Finish</h3>
      <div itemprop="text">
        <p>Faucets come in a variety of finishes such as chrome, brushed nickel, matte black, and bronze. Consider matching your faucet to your sink, countertop, or cabinet hardware to create a cohesive look that complements your space.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div itemprop="step" itemScope itemType="https://schema.org/HowToStep">
      <h3 itemprop="name">2. Mounting Options</h3>
      <div itemprop="text">
        <p>Choose a faucet that suits your sink's configuration. Deck-mounted faucets are common on sinks and countertops, while wall-mounted faucets are ideal for vessel sinks or modern designs. Knowing your installation setup ensures a seamless upgrade.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div itemprop="step" itemScope itemType="https://schema.org/HowToStep">
      <h3 itemprop="name">3. Handle Types</h3>
      <div itemprop="text">
        <p>Decide between different handle configurations:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>Single-handle: Easy to use, great for quick temperature adjustments</li>
          <li>Double-handle: Traditional look, separate hot and cold controls</li>
          <li>Touchless: Modern convenience, improved hygiene</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div itemprop="step" itemScope itemType="https://schema.org/HowToStep">
      <h3 itemprop="name">4. Features and Functions</h3>
      <div itemprop="text">
        <p>Consider useful features like:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>Pull-down sprayers</li>
          <li>Touch activation</li>
          <li>Water filtration options</li>
          <li>Temperature memory</li>
          <li>Pause function</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div itemprop="step" itemScope itemType="https://schema.org/HowToStep">
      <h3 itemprop="name">5. Water Efficiency</h3>
      <div itemprop="text">
        <p>Look for faucets with:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>WaterSense certification</li>
          <li>Flow restrictors</li>
          <li>Aerators</li>
          <li>Low-flow options</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
