### A faucet leak is small—until it isn’t.

A annoying drip can aggravate and waste water—but mostly aggravate.
A leak at the base can ruin your counters and cabinets.
Low pressure or a loose handle can be the early warning that something’s failing.

We’ll find the real cause, fix it cleanly, and leave you with a faucet you can trust.
If you’re considering an upgrade while we’re there:
[choosing the right faucet](/articles/choosing-the-right-faucet/)
</div>

<div class="two-column">
## What to Do Right Now

- If the leak is under the sink or at the shutoff, turn off the angle stop(s).
- If you can’t stop the leak, shut off water to the home and call.
- Avoid over-tightening handles—many leaks come from worn parts, not “not tight enough.”

If you want help today, call: [707-200-8350](tel:7072008350)
