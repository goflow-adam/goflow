### A dishwasher leak can ruin cabinets and flooring. Let’s stop it fast.

When a dishwasher leaks, won’t drain, or isn’t cleaning, the problem usually isn’t the machine—it’s the connections, drain path, or a failed seal.
We’ll diagnose what’s actually happening, protect your kitchen, and give you a clear path forward.

If you’re ready, call us and we’ll make your problem ours.
</div>

<div class="two-column">
## What to Do Right Now (Before the Damage Spreads)

- If there’s active leaking, stop the cycle and shut off the dishwasher water supply (usually under the sink).
- If water is pooling, dry it up and don’t keep running the unit “to test it.”
- If you smell burning or see tripped breakers, stop and get the electrical checked.

If it’s urgent, call: [707-200-8350](tel:7072008350)
