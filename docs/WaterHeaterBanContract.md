Pre-2027 Water Heater Reservation & Installation Agreement

This Agreement is entered into between:
GoFlow Plumbing ("Contractor")andCustomer ("Customer")
1. Program Overview
This Agreement secures Customer’s participation in Contractor’s Pre-2027 Water Heater Reservation & Installation Program ("Program"). The Program is designed to provide Customer with access to a compliant gas water heater and future installation services in light of anticipated regulatory restrictions.
2. Program Fee
Customer agrees to pay a $999 Program Fee.
The Program Fee is:
	•	A non-refundable program fee (subject to cancellation rights below)
	•	Not a deposit for future installation
	•	Consideration for participation in the Program, including equipment reservation, storage, and administrative services
3. Equipment Reservation & Storage
3.1 Contractor will:
	•	Purchase a residential gas water heater manufactured prior to January 1, 2027
	•	Assign the unit specifically to Customer
	•	Provide documentation and/or photographic confirmation of the reserved unit
3.2 Contractor retains ownership of the equipment until installation or transfer under Section 10.
3.3 Contractor will store the unit at its facility.
3.4 Contractor reserves the right to substitute the reserved unit with a functionally equivalent unit if the original unit becomes unavailable due to damage, recall, regulatory change, or other circumstances beyond Contractor’s control.
4. Definition of “Like-for-Like” Replacement
Installation will include a functionally equivalent gas water heater meeting the following general criteria:
	•	Same nominal capacity (40 or 50 gallon)
	•	Comparable or greater BTU rating
	•	Comparable or greater efficiency
	•	Compatible venting type (or improved where feasible)
Final equipment selection is subject to:
	•	Physical fit at the installation site
	•	Code compliance at time of installation
	•	Availability of compliant equipment
5. Installation Terms
5.1 Standard Installation Price
Customer is entitled to a base installation price of $3,999, subject to the conditions below.
5.2 Pre-Installation Inspection
Prior to installation, Contractor will perform a site inspection.
If Contractor determines that conditions deviate from a standard replacement, Contractor may:
	•	Provide a revised estimate based on site-specific conditions
	•	Require Customer approval before proceeding
5.3 Standard Installation Assumptions
The base installation price assumes:
	•	Same installation location
	•	Adequate access
	•	Existing code compliance
	•	No required venting modifications
	•	No structural or repair work
5.4 Additional Costs
Customer is responsible for additional costs arising from:
	•	Code upgrades
	•	Access limitations or changes (minimum $200 access fee may apply)
	•	Venting modifications
	•	Water damage or repairs
	•	Any non-standard conditions identified during inspection
6. Installation Trigger & Timing
6.1 Installation may be requested by Customer at any time while:
	•	The existing water heater remains in service, and
	•	This Agreement has not expired
6.2 Customer must notify Contractor when replacement is desired.
6.3 Contractor will schedule installation based on availability and urgency.
6.4 Contractor is not liable for:
	•	Loss of use
	•	Property damage
	•	Consequential damages arising from system failure or delays
7. Term & Expiration
This Agreement is valid for 10 years from the date of signing.
If unused at expiration:
	•	The Agreement terminates
	•	The Program Fee is forfeited
8. Cancellation
8.1 Customer may cancel within three (3) business days for a full refund.
8.2 After three (3) business days:
	•	Contractor retains 50% of the Program Fee ($499.50)
	•	Remaining balance will be refunded
9. Transferability
9.1 This Agreement may be transferred to a new homeowner of the property.
9.2 Transfer requires:
	•	Written request
	•	New owner information
	•	Payment of a $150 transfer fee
9.3 Contractor reserves the right to verify eligibility prior to approval.
10. Customer Escape Option
Customer may terminate participation and take possession of the reserved water heater by paying an escape fee equal to 50% of the Program Fee ($499.50).
Upon exercising this option:
	•	Customer assumes full ownership of the unit
	•	Customer is responsible for transportation
	•	All installation rights and obligations under this Agreement are terminated
11. Code & Regulatory Changes
11.1 Customer is responsible for costs associated with code-required upgrades at time of installation.
11.2 If installation of the reserved unit becomes illegal or infeasible due to regulatory changes:
	•	The Program Fee will convert to a $999 credit toward a code-compliant installation
12. Inventory Availability
If Contractor is unable to procure a compliant “like-for-like” unit despite reasonable efforts:
	•	This Agreement will be canceled
	•	Customer will receive a full refund
13. Warranty
13.1 Contractor will provide a workmanship and material warranty equivalent to the manufacturer’s standard warranty, beginning at time of installation.
13.2 Contractor’s total liability under this warranty is limited to:
	•	The cost of replacement equipment and
	•	Standard installation labor
13.3 Warranty does not cover:
	•	Misuse
	•	Neglect
	•	External damage
	•	Non-covered manufacturer exclusions
14. Maintenance Disclaimer
Any maintenance or inspections provided are advisory only.
Contractor does not guarantee:
	•	Performance
	•	Lifespan
	•	Prevention of failure
Customer acknowledges that system failure may occur at any time.
15. Limitation of Liability
Contractor shall not be liable for:
	•	Water damage
	•	Property damage
	•	Loss of use
	•	Indirect or consequential damage
