    ## Finding the Right Water Heater for Petaluma's Hard Water

    Not every water heater survives Petaluma's mineral-heavy water equally well.
    Here's what we recommend based on decades of local experience:
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### Standard Tanks
    The familiar choice—affordable upfront and easy to maintain. Gas, electric, or hybrid.
    **The catch in Petaluma Plumbing:** Hard water deposits build up fast. Without annual flushing,
    you'll lose 2-3 years of lifespan. We can set you up on a maintenance schedule.
    30-80 gallon sizes available. Expect 8-12 years with proper care.
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### On-Demand Tankless
    No tank means no sediment sitting in standing water. That's a real advantage here.
    Tankless water heaters often outlast tanks in Petaluma by 5+ years because mineral buildup
    affects them differently. Our tankless water heater service covers installation, repair, and maintenance.
    Endless hot water, wall-mounted, 20+ year lifespan.
    Requires gas line and venting upgrades—we handle all of it.
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### Heat Pump Units
    Pull heat from the air instead of generating it—2-3x more efficient.
    Petaluma's mild climate is ideal for these. They struggle below 40°F,
    but we rarely see those temperatures. Added bonus: they dehumidify your space.
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### Solar-Assisted Systems
    Petaluma averages 260 sunny days per year—plenty to make solar worthwhile.
    Federal and state incentives cut the upfront cost significantly.
    We always include backup heating for foggy weeks and winter.
  </div>
