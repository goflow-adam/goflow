    ## How to Check for a Gas Leak (Safely)

    A simple, safe way to check your gas line for leaks is the **soapy water test**.
    This test works well because gas lines are not a high pressure system.
    Don't rely just on your sense of smell. We sometimes can't smell the gas from a leak
    because it is diluted by other odors in the air or it is just too faint to smell.
    ### How it works:

    1. Mix a small amount of dish soap with water.
    2. Apply it to visible gas line connections using a sponge or spray bottle.
    3. Watch for bubbles forming.

    **If bubbles appear**, that indicates gas escaping and the system should be checked by a professional.

    > **Important:** Never use a flame to check for gas leaks
    unless you want to win a [Darwin Award](https://darwinawards.com/).

    If you ever smell gas, leave the area immediately and contact your gas provider
    or emergency services according to local guidelines.
