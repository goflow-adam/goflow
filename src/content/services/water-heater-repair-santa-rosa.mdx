    ## Finding the Right Water Heater for Your Santa Rosa Home

    From 1900s Victorians to 2020s rebuilds, Santa Rosa has it all. Here's what works
    in each situation based on our decades of local experience:
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### Classic Tank Heaters
    The tried-and-true option. Gas, electric, or hybrid in 30-80 gallon sizes.
    **Why they work in Santa Rosa:** Most homes here have the space, and the city's
    water quality is gentler on tanks than some neighboring areas. Annual flushing
    keeps them running 8-12 years. We size based on your family's actual patterns.
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### Tankless (Continuous Flow)
    Endless hot water from a wall-mounted unit. Great for Santa Rosa's larger families
    and homes where the garage is already full. 20+ year lifespan, 30% energy savings.
    Post-fire rebuilds in Fountaingrove often choose tankless—we've installed dozens there.
  </div>
  <div class="two-column">
    ### Heat Pump Water Heaters
    2-3x more efficient by pulling heat from ambient air. Santa Rosa's mild climate
    is ideal—these units struggle below 40°F, but we rarely see those temperatures.
    Popular with efficiency-minded homeowners. Bonus: they dehumidify your space.
  </div>
