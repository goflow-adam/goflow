### When the disposal acts up, the whole kitchen feels it.

A disposal that hums, jams, leaks, or clogs can stop your sink from working and create odors fast.
We’ll troubleshoot the cause, fix it cleanly, and tell you honestly whether repair makes sense—or
replacement is the smarter call.

If you’re considering a new unit, this guide can help you choose:
[replace your in-sink disposal](/articles/garbage-disposal-choices/)
</div>

<div class="two-column">
## What to Do Right Now

- If it’s jammed, **don’t keep flipping the switch**—that can burn up the unit.
- If it’s leaking under the sink, stop using the disposal and dry the cabinet.
- If you’re going to check for a jam, **turn off power first** (switch and/or breaker).

Need help today? Call: [707-200-8350](tel:7072008350)
